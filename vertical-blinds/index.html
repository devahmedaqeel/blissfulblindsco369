Vertical Blinds

The perfect solution for large windows, sliding doors, and conservatories. Rotate slats 180 degrees to filter sun rays, maintain daylight ventilation, or block out unwanted views completely.

Book Home Visit Explore Fabric Swatches

Why Choose Vertical Blinds?

Exceptional thermal control, low maintenance, and perfect formatting for wider spans.

Thermal Efficiency

Helps regulate your room's temperature by reflecting summer heat waves and trapping warmth against window glass during freezing winters.

Ideal for Wide Windows

Extremely lightweight louvers make it easy and effortless to draw panels across expansive glass doors, bay shapes, or commercial storefronts.

Low Maintenance

Since the louvers hang vertically, they do not collect dust like horizontal formats. Clean them in seconds with a damp cloth.
