How Much Do Made-to-Measure Blinds Cost? A Guide to Our Pricing & Package Offers

Real advertised prices, what actually affects a bespoke quote, and exactly how our deposit and payment process works — no guesswork, no invented numbers.

Get a Free Quote Jump to Package Prices

Because every blind we make is bespoke and custom-fitted to your exact windows, we can't give you an accurate price over the phone without measurements — the true cost of any order depends on your window size, product type, material, colour, quantity, and whether you want motorisation. What we can tell you upfront are the real, fixed prices on our current package offers, exactly what factors influence a bespoke quote, and how the deposit and payment process works once you're ready to order.

What Affects Bespoke Pricing

Outside of the fixed packages above, every quote is personalised. Rather than guess, our advisors work out your price using the following confirmed factors, in person, during your free consultation:

  • Material

  • Installation

Deposit & Payment, Explained

Once you're happy with your quote, a minimum deposit of 50% is required to confirm and place your order. The remaining balance is then settled at fitting, when an adult over 18 must be present. We accept payment by Bank Transfer or PayPal only — cheque payments are not accepted.

If you find the same blinds, in the same size and specification, cheaper elsewhere, our Lowest Price Guarantee means we'll match it — and try to beat it. Ready for an accurate, no-obligation price for your own windows? Book your free home consultation below.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can you give me an exact price over the phone?
No — all our blinds are bespoke and custom-made, so we cannot provide accurate quotations over the phone without dimensions. Pricing depends on window size, product type, material, colour, quantity, motorisation, and installation, so an accurate price only comes from a free, in-home consultation.
What's included in the package offers?
Each package includes the blinds themselves, professional measuring, and precision fitting. For example, the 4 Vertical Blinds Package (£199 for 4 windows) and 4 Roller Blinds (from £239 for 4 windows) both include measuring and fitting, and every package carries our standard 1-year product guarantee.
What deposit is required and how do I pay?
A minimum deposit of 50% is required to confirm and place an order, with the remaining balance settled at fitting when an adult over 18 must be present. We accept Bank Transfer or PayPal — cheque payments are not accepted.
Do the packages include fitting?
Yes. All of our package offers, including the Full House Blinds Bundle and the Conservatory Vertical Blinds package, include measuring, supply, and precision fitting as standard — there are no hidden fees added on top.
