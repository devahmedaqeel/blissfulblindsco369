Our Window Blinds Range

Browse our full selection of premium, made-to-measure window blinds. Each product is custom manufactured and professionally fitted to perfectly suit your windows, rooms, and style.

Roller Blinds

Roller blinds are a modern, simple, and versatile blind suitable for any size of window. They are the most popular type of window blind, suitable for any size of window in the home or office. Roller blinds are a practical, functional and elegant solution to control light and privacy. They are available in a wide range of fabrics, colours, and patterns to suit any interior décor.

  • Available in blackout, sunscreen & decorative fabrics
  • Suitable for kitchens, bathrooms & bedrooms
  • Custom made to your exact window measurements
View Roller Blinds Book Consultation

Vertical Blinds

Vertical blinds are a modern, simple, and highly adjustable blind suitable for any size of window. Vertical blinds are the most popular type of patio door blind. They are a derivation of the horizontal Venetian blind and work on a similar principle — individual slats hang vertically from a track and can be rotated or drawn to one or both sides to control light and privacy.

  • Perfect for large windows and patio doors
  • Wide choice of colours, fabrics & textures
  • Full-length slat control for precise light management
View Vertical Blinds Book Consultation

Perfect Fit Blinds

Perfect Fit blinds are a revolutionary no-drill blind system that clips directly into your UPVC window frame — no screws, no drilling, no damage. The blind sits neatly within the window recess and moves with the window when it's opened or closed. They are available in roller, pleated, and venetian styles and are the ideal choice for modern double-glazed windows.

  • No drilling required — clips into UPVC frame
  • Available in roller, pleated & venetian styles
  • Moves with the window — no tangling
View Perfect Fit Blinds Book Consultation

Vision Blinds (Day & Night)

Vision blinds — also known as day and night blinds — combine the benefits of a roller blind and a sheer curtain in one innovative product. Twin layers of alternating translucent and opaque fabric bands slide past each other to offer you full flexibility: open for a bright, airy feel; align for a soft, filtered light; or close completely for total privacy and blackout.

  • Combines sheer & blackout in one blind
  • Three levels of light control from one product
  • Contemporary look for any modern interior
View Vision Blinds Book Consultation

Pleated Blinds

Pleated blinds are a versatile, compact window covering with a neat, concertina-style fold. They are ideal for roof lights, skylights, conservatories, and any window where space is at a premium. Available in a wide range of fabrics including thermal, blackout, and light-filtering options, they provide an elegant solution with a minimal footprint when raised.

  • Perfect for skylights & conservatory windows
  • Thermal & blackout fabric options available
  • Compact folding design — minimal stack height
View Pleated Blinds Book Consultation

Roman Blinds

Roman blinds are a classic and elegant window covering made from a single panel of fabric that folds into neat horizontal pleats when raised. They offer a softer, more luxurious look than a roller blind and bring warmth, texture, and a premium feel to any room. Available in a huge range of fabrics, patterns, and colours — from light-filtering sheers to full blackout linings.

  • Huge choice of fabrics, patterns & linings
  • Adds warmth & elegance to living rooms & bedrooms
  • Available with blackout or thermal lining
View Roman Blinds Book Consultation

Wooden Blinds

Wooden blinds bring a natural warmth and organic beauty to any interior. Made from real high-grade basswood or faux wood, they add a premium design accent to any room. The horizontal slats can be tilted to control light levels and privacy, while the warm tones and natural grain complement both contemporary and traditional interiors beautifully.

  • Real basswood & faux wood options
  • Natural finish complements all interior styles
  • Wide range of stains, paints & finishes

Our bespoke plantation shutters are crafted from real wood and faux wood in a huge range of styles and finishes. They provide outstanding insulation, noise reduction, privacy control, and add real structural value and kerb appeal to any property.

Blackout Blinds

Blackout blinds are an essential choice for bedrooms, nurseries, and media rooms where total darkness is required. Featuring premium light-blocking lining, they completely eliminate light penetration through the fabric, ensuring a perfect night's sleep or an immersive viewing experience at any time of day. Available in roller, roman, and pleated styles.

  • 100% light blockout — no gaps
  • Essential for nurseries & bedrooms
  • Available in roller, roman & pleated styles

Conservatory Blinds

Conservatory blinds are a high-performance window shading solution designed specifically for glass-roofed sunrooms and conservatory extensions. Regulate heat, block out harsh glare, and enjoy your conservatory comfortably in both summer heat and winter cold. Fitted with no-drill clips that protect your window framing warranty.

  • Keeps room cool in summer & warm in winter
  • Protects internal furniture from UV fading
  • Perfect Fit no-drill frame clip-in options

Skylight Blinds

Skylight blinds are custom-fit window covers manufactured specifically for roof windows and loft light openings (including Velux, Fakro, and Keylite frames). Fitted snugly inside side channels to prevent angled light leak, they provide optimal light control, privacy, and outstanding thermal insulation for top-floor attic rooms.

  • Perfect fit for Velux, Fakro, and roof light systems
  • Available in light-filtering and full blackout formats
  • Extension control rod or remote motorized control

Completely waterproof and warp-resistant, they are the ideal window dressing for high-humidity areas like kitchens and bathrooms. Enjoy timeless style that is incredibly easy to clean.

  • 100% waterproof and moisture resistant

Cellular Blinds

Comfort in every layer. Cellular Blinds are designed with advanced honeycomb technology that traps pockets of air. This forms an active thermal barrier at your window pane to reduce heat loss, block out harsh drafts, and help lower domestic energy bills.

  • Thermal insulation for summer cooling & winter warmth
  • Noise reduction honeycomb structure
  • Energy efficient design reduces utility bills

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about prices, installations, or scheduling appointments? Find our transparent answers below.

How much do made-to-measure blinds cost?
All our blinds are bespoke and custom-made. Consequently, we cannot provide accurate quotations over the phone without dimensions. However, we offer home consultations where we measure up and give an instant quote. The average visit lasts 30 to 60 minutes.
What deposit is required?
A minimum deposit of 50% is required at the time you confirm and place your order. This allows us to secure raw materials and initiate custom manufacturing of your blinds.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept payments via Bank Transfer or PayPal. Please note that we do not accept cheque payments. Our team will provide full payment details upon confirmation of your order.
How long will I wait for my blinds to be fitted?
Your blinds will be custom manufactured and ready to fit in 7 to 14 working days from placing your order. Fitting timelines can vary from 1 to 4 weeks depending on the type of blinds and seasonal demand spikes.
Do I need to take down my old window blinds?
If you are able to take your old blinds down and clear the window area before our fitting team arrives, it helps speed up the installation process. However, if you are unable to, our fitters will gladly do this on the day.
Do I need to be present for the installation?
Yes, someone over the age of 18 must be present during fitting to approve the installation and settle the remaining 50% balance.
Are home visits completely free?
Yes! All home measurements, consultations, and fitting services are 100% free of charge. There are no hidden fees added to your product pricing.
Do you offer evening or weekend appointments?
Yes, we offer consultations after 6 PM and on weekends upon customer request (available in selected locations). Please fill out the booking form or call us on 01733 853037 to coordinate a slot.
Are all of your window blinds child safe?
Absolutely. In compliance with British Blind & Shutter Association (BBSA) legislation, all blinds containing looped cords are supplied with custom tensioning safety devices to eliminate hazard risks.
Can I see fabric samples in person?
Yes. During your in-home consultation, our design advisors carry full swatch books containing hundreds of fabric options, colors, and textures for you to feel and inspect against your wall colorings.
Do you offer a wide variety of colors and fabrics?
Absolutely! We offer a massive collection of over 300+ colors, materials, patterns, and fabric styles. During our free home visit, our advisor will bring physical swatch books showing our full range of whites, greys, wood stains, textured patterns, and vibrant accents so you can find the perfect match.
