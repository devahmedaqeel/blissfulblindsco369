Venetian Blinds

Enjoy absolute control of light filtration and privacy with our range of horizontal slatted Venetian blinds. Available in moisture-resistant Faux Wood, solid Hardwood, and sleek, contemporary Aluminium.

Book Home Visit Browse Materials

Why Choose Venetian Blinds?

Durable, moisture-resistant slatted window treatments custom made for your windows.

Adjustable Slat Widths

Select from 25mm, 35mm, 50mm, and 63mm slat width configurations to achieve the perfect depth, look, and density for your glazing size.

100% Moisture Proof

Our Faux Wood collection behaves like solid timber but is made from PVC, preventing warping or cracking in damp bathrooms or hot kitchens.

Affordable Luxury

Get the look of solid hardwood shutters or timber shades at a fraction of the cost, complete with stylish woven tape additions.

Material Collections

Choose the perfect timber stain, neutral paint coat, or sleek metal glaze for your rooms.

Faux Wood

Perfect combination of style and resilience. Features co-ordinated valances, heavy-duty bottom rails, and matching cord controls.

Hardwood

Made from renewably sourced, FSC-certified timber. Features premium hand-painted coats and rich, natural grain stains.

Aluminium Slats

Lightweight, incredibly tough, and modern. Offers sleek metallic profiles suited to workspaces, office desks, and bathrooms.
