Vision Blinds

Also known as Day & Night Blinds. Vision blinds feature two layers of horizontally striped sheer and opaque fabric. Sliding these stripes past each other allows millimeter-precise adjustments to filter daylight, maintain privacy, or darken your room completely.

Book Home Visit Explore Fabric Swatches

Why Choose Vision Blinds?

Truly flexible light adjustment, thermal insulation, and stunning modern aesthetics.

Shifting Layer Shading

Instantly shifts from fully transparent daylight strips to total blockout alignment in a single pull, eliminating the need to raise the entire blind.

Natural Insulation

The dual-layer structure traps pockets of air between fabrics, forming an effective barrier against cold window glass and summer heat.

Motorization Option

Available with motorized controls. Raise, lower, and align your stripes with a simple press of a button from your sofa or bed.
