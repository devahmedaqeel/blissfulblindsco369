This guide only restates facts already published elsewhere in the knowledge base
(see `products.md` for full detail on each product). Use it to match a customer's
stated need to a suitable product, then explain the match using the real feature that
justifies it.

## By room

- **Bathrooms / kitchens (moisture-prone)**: Faux Wood Venetian Blinds (100%
  moisture-proof, won't warp), Waterproof PVC Roller Blinds, Cotton Flower fabric
  Vertical Blinds (moisture-proof)
- **Bedrooms / nurseries**: Blackout Blinds or blackout-lined Roller/Roman blinds
  (100% light blockout), cordless Perfect Fit or Pleated blinds (child safety)
- **Living rooms / dining**: Roman Blinds (warmth, texture), Wooden Blinds (natural
  finish), Vision Blinds (flexible light control)
- **Conservatories / skylights**: Pleated Blinds (compact fold, fits irregular/angled
  windows, thermal & blackout options), Perfect Fit Blinds (clips into UPVC frames,
  moves with the window)
- **Home offices**: Vision Blinds in Anthracite Capri (shields computer screens from
  glare), Venetian Blinds (precise tilt control)
- **Large windows / patio & sliding doors**: Vertical Blinds (full-length slat
  control), Tracked Window Shutters (fold back 180°)
- **Bay windows**: Bay Window Shutters (bespoke angles for English bay windows)

## By privacy need

- Total privacy/blackout: Blackout Blinds, Vision Blinds (closed/aligned position),
  Perfect Fit Blinds
- Adjustable/partial privacy: Venetian Blinds, Vertical Blinds, Vision Blinds
  (aligned position for filtered light)

## By light control

- Full flexible control (sheer to blackout in one product): Vision (Day & Night) Blinds
- Precise directional control: Venetian Blinds (tilt), Vertical Blinds (rotate)
- Top-down/bottom-up flexible control: Pleated Blinds

## By child/pet safety

- Perfect Fit Blinds — 100% cordless, no hanging cords or loops
- Pleated Blinds — cordless options available
- Any blind with looped cords — all blinds with looped cords are supplied with custom
  tensioning safety devices in compliance with BBSA legislation (see `faq.md`)

## By energy efficiency

- Pleated/Cellular Blinds — honeycomb structure traps air, thermal barrier against
  drafts
- Vision Blinds — dual-layer structure traps air pockets, natural insulation
- Window Shutters — can reduce heat loss by up to 30% at the window frame

## By interior style

- Contemporary/modern: Roller Blinds, Aluminium Venetian Blinds, Vision Blinds
- Classic/traditional: Roman Blinds, Wooden Blinds, Hardwood Venetian Blinds, Window
  Shutters
- Natural/organic: Wooden Blinds, Faux Wood Venetian Blinds, Raffia Flax pleated fabric

## By window type

- UPVC double-glazed windows, doors, conservatories: Perfect Fit Blinds (no drilling,
  moves with the window)
- Skylights/roof lights: Pleated Blinds
- Large glazing/patio doors: Vertical Blinds
- Bay windows: Bay Window Shutters

## By budget

The site does not publish per-window pricing for made-to-measure products (see
`pricing-policy.md`) — bespoke quotes depend on measurements and fabric/material
choice. It does publish fixed prices for specific bundle offers (see
`pricing-policy.md`), which the chatbot can mention if relevant to a customer asking
about value packages.
