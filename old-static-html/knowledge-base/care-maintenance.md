The site does not publish a dedicated cleaning/care guide. The only confirmed
maintenance-related facts, taken from individual product descriptions, are:

- **Vertical Blinds**: "Low Maintenance" — because the louvers hang vertically, they
  do not collect dust like horizontal blinds; the site says to clean them "in seconds
  with a damp cloth."
- **Waterproof PVC Roller Blinds**: "Extremely easy to clean and wipe down,"
  moisture-resistant, ideal for high-humidity areas like bathrooms and kitchens.
- **Window Shutters**: "Allergy Friendly" — incredibly easy to dust and clean; unlike
  heavy fabric drapes, shutters don't harbour pollen or dust mites, which the site says
  can aid asthma sufferers.
- **Faux Wood Venetian Blinds**: 100% moisture-proof, won't warp or crack in damp
  environments (implying easier upkeep than real wood in bathrooms/kitchens).
