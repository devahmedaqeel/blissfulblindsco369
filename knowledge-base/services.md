## Confirmed services

- Free in-home consultation and quotation
- Free professional measuring
- Free professional fitting/installation
- Supply and installation of: Roller Blinds, Vertical Blinds, Venetian Blinds
  (wood/faux wood/aluminium), Perfect Fit Blinds, Vision (Day & Night) Blinds,
  Pleated/Cellular Blinds, Roman Blinds, Wooden Blinds, Blackout Blinds, Window
  Shutters (plantation shutters)
- Motorised/remote-control operation is offered as an option on some product lines
  (explicitly mentioned for Roller Blinds and Vision Blinds)
- Commercial and landlord blinds packages (see below)

## Commercial blinds & shutters

Blissful Blinds Co 369 supplies commercial-grade, heavy-duty window solutions for
offices, boardrooms, and rental/landlord properties:

- Over 50 premium fire-retardant (FR) fabrics in a specialised commercial collection
- Heavy-duty Vertical, Roller, Venetian, and Pleated blinds designed for high-traffic use
- Full safety compliance: child-safe mechanisms and comprehensive Risk Assessments provided
- Commercial client loyalty discounts on repeat installations and property developments
- For offices: helps minimise monitor glare and maintain climate control; the company
  manages the project from measurement to rapid, non-disruptive installation
- For landlords: durable, affordable blinds packages for property management companies
  and individual landlords, supplied direct from the manufacturer

## Services NOT confirmed on the site

Do not confirm these unless the customer is told to check with the team — they are not
described anywhere on the current site:

- Standalone curtains (only mentioned once, as a descriptive comparison for how Vision
  blinds work — curtains are not sold as a separate product)
- A dedicated blind repair service, pricing, or turnaround time (see `repairs.md`)
- Skylight blinds, Conservatory blinds, or Faux Wood blinds as separately described
  products — these appear only as selectable options in the booking form's "Type of
  Blinds" list, with no dedicated description of their own. Conservatory blinds are
  covered by the "Conservatory Blinds Package" offer (see `pricing-policy.md`), which
  is fulfilled using pleated or vertical fabrics.
