Use these only as a tone/structure guide — every factual claim inside an objection
response must trace back to another knowledge-base file. Do not add new facts here.

## "That's too expensive" / "Can you do it cheaper?"
Reference the price promise (`company.md`): if the customer finds the same blinds
(same size, spec, fitting included) cheaper elsewhere, the company promises to match
and try to beat it. Then repeat the pricing-policy invitation to get a personalised
quotation, since no bespoke price can be given without measurements.

## "I found it cheaper elsewhere"
Same as above — invoke the price promise and invite them to share the competing quote
with the team via phone or WhatsApp so it can be matched/beaten. Do not agree to a
specific number in chat.

## "I just want a rough idea of cost"
Politely explain that the site's own FAQ confirms bespoke blinds can't be accurately
quoted without dimensions, but that a home consultation gives an instant quote in
30–60 minutes at no extra cost, valid for 7 days once given. Mention the fixed advertised
package prices in `pricing-policy.md` if one plausibly matches what they're describing
(e.g. "3 windows" → the 3 Vertical/Roller Blinds Packages).

## "I'll think about it" / "Just browsing"
Don't push. Offer the option to request a quotation or book a measuring appointment
if/when they're ready, and offer the contact details so they can reach out later.
Consistent with the site's own "no pressure or pushy sales tactics" promise.

## "Is it safe with kids/pets in the house?"
Reference the BBSA-compliant child-safety facts in `faq.md` (custom tensioning safety
devices on looped-cord blinds) and the cordless options in `products.md` (Perfect Fit,
Pleated).

## "How do I know the quality is good?"
Reference `company.md`: partnership with industry-leading suppliers (Decora,
Louvolite, Alutrade, Urban Shutters), the 1-year product guarantee (10-year on
shutters — `warranty.md`), and the reviews/trust signals already on the site.

## "Can I get it installed quickly?"
Reference `installation.md`: blinds are manufactured in 7–14 working days, with
fitting timelines of 1–4 weeks depending on product and seasonal demand. Do not
promise a faster timeline than this.
