## The 6-step process (as published on the site)

1. **Get in Touch** — Call or submit the online form. The team coordinates and
   schedules an in-home consultation at a time that fits the customer's schedule.
2. **We Come to You** — The showroom comes to the customer's home. An expert advisor
   takes exact, professional measurements of the windows.
3. **Choose Your Blinds** — Browse a catalogue of hundreds of styles, colours,
   textures, and fabrics. The advisor helps select options to suit requirements and
   budget.
4. **No-Obligation Quote** — The customer receives a competitive quotation based on
   measurements and fabric choices. The quote is valid for 7 days.
5. **Order Your Blinds** — Once the quote is confirmed, the blinds go into
   manufacturing. Custom blinds are aimed to be ready in 7–14 days.
6. **We Fit Your Blinds** — Once manufactured, a fitting appointment is scheduled. A
   qualified professional fitter installs the blinds, conducts a quality safety check,
   and cleans up completely.

## Installation FAQ facts

- Fitting timelines can vary from 1 to 4 weeks depending on the type of blinds and
  seasonal demand.
- Customers don't have to take their old blinds down themselves — the fitting team
  will do it on the day if needed (though doing it in advance speeds things up).
- Someone over the age of 18 must be present during fitting, to approve the
  installation and settle the remaining 50% balance (see `pricing-policy.md` for the
  deposit structure).
- Measuring and fitting are carried out by the company's own team, not third-party
  contractors, based on the site's description of "our fitters" and "our expert
  advisor."
