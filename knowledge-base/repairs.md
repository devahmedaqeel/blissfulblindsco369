## Important: no repairs content is published on the site

A full search of every live page found no mention of a blind repair service,
turnaround time, call-out fee, or spare-parts policy. This is different from
installation of new blinds, which is fully documented (see `installation.md`).

## How the chatbot should handle repair enquiries

Do not claim a specific repair service, price, or turnaround time exists. Use the
standard "I don't have confirmed information about that" response (see
`sales-playbook.md`), then invite the customer to contact the team directly by phone
or WhatsApp (see `contact.md`) so the team can advise whether they can help with a
repair. Treat a repair enquiry as buying intent worth capturing as a lead (see
`common-objections.md` and `sales-playbook.md`).
