## Roller Blinds
A modern, simple, versatile blind suitable for any window size, in the home or office.
A practical, functional, elegant solution to control light and privacy.

- Available in blackout, sunscreen & decorative fabrics
- Suitable for kitchens, bathrooms & bedrooms
- Custom made to exact window measurements
- Operation choices: standard spring-assisted, chain-driven, or fully motorised remote
  control — motorised is good for tall or hard-to-reach skylight windows
- Material types: Standard fabrics (wide range of textures/prints/colours), Blackout
  lining (total light block — good for nurseries/children's rooms/bedrooms), Waterproof
  PVC (easy to wipe clean, moisture-resistant — good for bathrooms/kitchens)
- Page tagline: "Stylish & Practical"

## Vertical Blinds
A modern, highly adjustable blind, the most popular type of patio-door blind. A
derivation of the horizontal Venetian blind — individual slats hang vertically from a
track and rotate or draw to one or both sides to control light and privacy.

- Perfect for large windows and patio doors
- Wide choice of colours, fabrics & textures
- Full-length slat control for precise light management
- Thermal efficiency: helps regulate room temperature (reflects summer heat, traps
  winter warmth)
- Low maintenance: vertical louvers don't collect dust like horizontal blinds — clean
  with a damp cloth
- Fabric lines mentioned: Windsor Forest Pine (standard woven texture, natural tones),
  Argent Mercury (premium blackout fabric, textured grey — good for study rooms/
  nurseries), Cotton Flower (pure white, floral detail, moisture-proof — good for
  bathrooms/kitchens)
- Page tagline: "Classic & Adjustable"


## Perfect Fit Blinds
A no-drill blind system that clips directly into a UPVC window frame — no screws, no
drilling, no damage. Sits neatly in the window recess and moves with the window when
opened/closed. Designed specifically for UPVC windows, doors, and conservatories; clips
between the glass and rubber gasket via spring steel clips.

- No drilling required — clips into UPVC frame, leaves sills completely clear
- Available in roller, pleated & venetian styles
- Moves with the window — no tangling; ideal for tilt-and-turn windows, French doors,
  bi-folds
- 100% cordless & safe — no hanging cords or loops, safe for children and pets (secure
  outer frame)
- Does not void UPVC window warranty (clips in, no drilling)
- Page tagline: "Drill-Free & Seamless"

## Vision Blinds (Day & Night Blinds)
Combine the benefits of a roller blind and a sheer curtain in one product. Two layers of
horizontally striped sheer and opaque fabric slide past each other for millimetre-precise
adjustment: fully open for bright/airy, aligned for soft filtered light, or closed for
total privacy/blackout.

- Combines sheer & blackout in one blind
- Three levels of light control from one product
- Shifting layer shading — switch from transparent to blockout in a single pull, no need
  to raise the whole blind
- Natural insulation — dual-layer traps air pockets, barrier against cold/heat
- Motorisation option available (raise/lower/align by button)
- Fabric lines mentioned: Muted Neutrals (living rooms/kitchens/dining), Anthracite
  Capri (deep charcoal, good for shielding computer screens from glare in offices),
  Metallic Textures (woven fabrics with metallic threads, modern apartments/lounges)
- Page tagline: "Day & Night Controls"

## Pleated Blinds (also called Cellular Blinds)
A versatile, compact window covering with a neat, concertina-style fold. Ideal for roof
lights, skylights, conservatories, and windows where space is at a premium.

- Perfect for skylights & conservatory windows
- Thermal & blackout fabric options available
- Compact folding design — minimal stack height when raised
- Honeycomb cellular structure traps air pockets — thermal barrier against drafts, keeps
  homes warm in winter/cool in summer, can reduce energy bills vs traditional slatted
  blinds
- Top-down bottom-up operation: raise from the bottom or lower from the top, keeping
  privacy while letting light filter in
- Cordless options available (child safety)
- Fits irregular windows: can be custom-cut for triangular attic shapes, curved bays,
  glass conservatory roofs
- Fabric lines mentioned: Festival Fern, Raffia Flax (textured linen-style weave),
  Duoshade Grey (double-layer cellular, reflects solar glare, made for the Perfect Fit
  frame system)
- Page tagline: "Unique & Energy Efficient"

## Window Shutters (Plantation Shutters)
The ultimate statement window covering. Bespoke plantation shutters crafted from real
wood and faux wood, individually measured and fitted with precision. Available in wood,
PVC, and security-metal builds.

- Real wood & faux wood options
- Adds long-term value to a property; excellent insulation & noise reduction
- Page highlights a 10-Year Product Warranty (longer than the standard 1-year guarantee
  quoted for blinds generally — see `warranty.md`)
- Eco-friendly barrier: can reduce heat loss by up to 30% at the window frame, lowering
  winter bills and keeping summer rooms cool
- Acoustic noise barrier: closed louvers reflect incoming sound waves, buffering street
  noise
- Allergy-friendly: easy to dust/clean, doesn't harbour pollen or dust mites like heavy
  fabric drapes
- Styles available: Full Height (most popular — full-length panels top to bottom), Cafe
  Style (covers only the lower half of the glass for street-level privacy with daylight
  from the top), Tier-on-Tier (upper/lower panels open independently), Tracked (top and
  bottom guide tracks, panels fold back 180° — good for patio sliders/bi-folds), Bay
  Window Shutters (bespoke angles for English bay windows)
- Page tagline: "10-Year Product Warranty"

## Cellular Blinds
Energy-efficient window coverings made with a unique honeycomb fabric pocket structure that traps air to form an active thermal barrier.

- High-performance insulation: blocks drafts, keeps rooms warm in winter and cool in summer
- Acoustic noise reduction: honeycomb design buffers street and outside noise
- Energy savings: lowers heat loss through windows to reduce utility bills
- Child safe: cordless by default or secure tensioned bottom bar controls
- Suited for bedrooms, nurseries, bi-folds, and conservatory windows
- Page tagline: "Comfort in Every Layer."
