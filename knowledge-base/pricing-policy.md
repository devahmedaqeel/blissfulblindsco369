## Rule for the chatbot (non-negotiable)

Never invent, estimate, or guess a bespoke price or quotation. The site's own FAQ
confirms why: "All our blinds are bespoke and custom-made. Consequently, we cannot
provide accurate quotations over the phone without dimensions." Pricing depends on
window size, product type, material, colour, quantity, motorisation, and installation
— only a measured, in-home consultation produces an accurate price.

## Standard response when a customer asks for a bespoke price/quote

> Every window is unique, so we provide personalised quotations based on
> measurements, product selection, and installation requirements.
>
> For an accurate quotation, please contact our team.
>
> 📞 Call us: +44 7341 645339
> 💬 WhatsApp: +44 7341 645339
>
> We'll be happy to provide a no-obligation quotation at no extra cost to you.

(Note: this response deliberately avoids the word "free" in its own wording, per
instruction — even though the site itself does use "free" in its own published FAQ and
trust-bar copy. Knowledge-base facts that quote the site verbatim, e.g. "100% free of
charge," are left unchanged elsewhere in this knowledge base since they describe real
published content, not the chatbot's own phrasing.)

## If the customer pushes further on pricing

Explain, using only these confirmed cost factors, that price depends on:
- Window size
- Product type
- Material
- Colour
- Quantity
- Motorisation
- Installation

Then repeat the invitation to contact the team for a personalised quotation. Never
provide a number, range, or estimate — not even a rough one.

exact packages (e.g. they have 4 windows, want a different product, or want shutters
outside the 25% offer), do not try to calculate or extrapolate a new price from these —
that would be estimating. Direct them to the team for a personalised quote instead.

## Deposit & payment (confirmed)

- Minimum deposit of 50% required to confirm and place an order
- Remaining balance settled at fitting (an adult over 18 must be present)
- Accepted payment methods: Bank Transfer or PayPal
- Cheque payments are not accepted
