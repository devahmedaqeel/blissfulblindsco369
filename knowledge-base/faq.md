## Pricing & Payment

**How much do made-to-measure blinds cost?**
All our blinds are bespoke and custom-made. Consequently, we cannot provide accurate
quotations over the phone without dimensions. However, we offer home consultations
where we measure up and give an instant quote. The average visit lasts 30 to 60 minutes.

**What deposit is required?**
A minimum deposit of 50% is required at the time you confirm and place your order. This
allows us to secure raw materials and initiate custom manufacturing of your blinds.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
We accept payments via Bank Transfer or PayPal. We do not accept cheque payments. Our
team will provide full payment details upon confirmation of your order.

## Fitting & Installation

**How long will I wait for my blinds to be fitted?**
Your blinds will be custom manufactured and ready to fit in 7 to 14 working days from
placing your order. Fitting timelines can vary from 1 to 4 weeks depending on the type
of blinds and seasonal demand spikes.

**Do I need to take down my old window blinds?**
If you are able to take your old blinds down and clear the window area before our
fitting team arrives, it helps speed up the installation process. However, if you are
unable to, our fitters will gladly do this on the day.

**Do I need to be present for the installation?**
Yes, someone over the age of 18 must be present during fitting to approve the
installation and settle the remaining 50% balance.

## Appointments

**Are home visits completely free?**
Yes! All home measurements, consultations, and fitting services are 100% free of
charge. There are no hidden fees added to your product pricing.

**Do you offer evening or weekend appointments?**
Yes, we offer consultations after 6 PM and on weekends upon customer request (available
in selected locations). Please fill out the booking form or call us on 01733 853037 to
coordinate a slot.

## Window Blinds

**Are all of your window blinds child safe?**
Absolutely. In compliance with British Blind & Shutter Association (BBSA) legislation,
all blinds containing looped cords are supplied with custom tensioning safety devices
to eliminate hazard risks.

**Can I see fabric samples in person?**
Yes. During your in-home consultation, our design advisors carry full swatch books
containing hundreds of fabric options, colors, and textures for you to feel and inspect
against your wall colourings.
