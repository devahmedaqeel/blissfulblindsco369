Perfect Fit Blinds

Designed specifically for UPVC windows, doors, and conservatories. These blinds clip directly between the glass and rubber gasket via spring steel clips. They move with the window and leave sills completely clear.

Book Home Visit Explore Swatches

Why Choose Perfect Fit Blinds?

No drilling, tidy styling, and integrated frame insulation.

Zero Drilling Required

Clips smoothly and securely into the frame structure without harming your UPVC warranty or creating dusty drill holes in plaster.

Moves with the Pane

Ideal for tilt-and-turn windows, French doors, and bi-folds. Open and shut your window vents without the blind rattling or getting in the way.

100% Cordless & Safe

Since they are contained within a secure outer frame, they have no hanging cords or loops, rendering them completely safe for children and pets.

Perfect Fit Styles

Choose from three integrated window blind styles manufactured inside the Perfect Fit clip-in system.

Roller Blinds

Neat, clean fabric profiles. Ideal for filtering glare and retaining heat across double-glazed conservatory panels.

Venetian Blinds

All the brightness control of slatted venetians within a tidy, clip-in border. Simple tension adjustments via bottom bar tilts.

Pleated Blinds

Honeycomb thermal cells that trap incoming drafts. Extremely compact, leaving sills clear for planters or ornaments.
