How It Works

Our made-to-measure service couldn’t be easier, more convenient and simpler. We provide and install your bespoke set of blinds tailored to fit the exact dimensions of your windows.

Get in touch

Step 2.

We’ve got just what you need! Our wide selection of stylish and modern blinds is perfect for any home. Plus, our made-to-measure blinds will fit perfectly in any window – no matter what size it is! And best of all, our blinds are child-safe and designed to keep your little ones safe and sound.

Vertical Blinds

Vertical blinds are a modern, simple and sleek option for any size of window. Vertical blinds are the remarkable kinds of blinds which are a derivation from the unique style of blinds. These when added to your room give you great décor and a flexible way to use as well as comfort and reliability.

Roller Blinds

Roller Blinds have a versatile, stylish and functional feature. These types of blinds are an easy and ideal way to make a difference in your home/office. These are used in offices for the very reason they portray sturdiness and authority. With string solid rollers as the covering materials these are highly durable.

Venetian Blinds - Faux Wood & Metal

Venetian blinds suit areas which are spacious and wide. Use of these makes sure your space looks wider than usual delivering giving your home a luxurious feel. The venetian blinds have multiple levers like vertical blinds thus letting you adjust the angle of the flaps to control light and air.

Roman Blinds

Roman blinds are suitable for almost any window and portrays the beauty your house deserves. Roman blinds are unique in their own way. These are made from light and very thin materials and have a single panel from top to bottom. Choose from our made to measure roman blinds to add that WOW factor.

Pleated Blinds

Pleated blinds combine the key elements of flexibility, design and performance. A versatile alternative featuring slim top and bottom rails that draw up to the top of the window neatly when not in use, pleated blinds take up less room compared to other types of blind, while still granting a room a smart, muted shading effect.

Wooden Blinds

Bring the natural beauty of the outdoors into your home or office with made to measure wooden blinds. The elegance and firmness of wood cannot be competed with by any material when it comes to blinds. For those who like peaceful and quiet surroundings it is the best addition to a room.

Blackout

Blackout blinds are perfect for providing complete privacy, block out sunlight as well as being functional and fashionable available in eye-catching colours. This is a great option fitted with our standard blackout lining, they completely obstruct any sunlight and prevent it from entering your room, granting you complete control of light and privacy and helping to ensure an excellent night’s sleep.

Window Blinds

Are your blinds child safe?

Yes, all our blinds are child safe. As a result of British Blind & Shutter Association (BBSA) legislation introduced in early 2014, the blind industry has altered its products to ensure that any blind featuring a hazardous looped cord is supplied with a child safety device. This device always keeps any looped cord tensioned preventing strangulation. There are also restrictions on the length of the cord too. Each member of our Customer Care team is happy to answer any questions you may have regarding child safety, so if you would like to know more please Contact Us or visit the Make It Safe & BBSA websites.

Do you offer free samples?

Yes certainly, upon your home consultation we bring a catalogue that has hundreds of different samples and fabrics for each blind type. We will be happy to chat through your options and give you prices on whichever blinds you require.

How long is the guarantee on your blinds?

We are so confident in our work and quality of our product; we offer a 12 month Guarantee on all our blinds. This means that we will cover all the parts and material which make up your blind. As well as, arranging a replacement like for like, if an issue occurs.

Can I wash my blinds?

No, unfortunately there are no washable fabric blinds, we do recommend washing your blinds. Other than cleaning them with a sponge and mild detergent and water solution.

Do you repair blinds?

If the blinds were made and supplied by us, then we will repair or replace them if they are still in warranty. As blinds are component based, it’s often more cost effective to replace the mechanism and in some cases the whole blind. We don’t repair blinds supplied by any other company only because our components may not be compatible with other components and vice versa.

Can I measure my windows myself?

Yes, you can, but it’s not usually recommended. Most stores will not be held liable if your custom window coverings do not fit due to imprecise measurements that you have done yourself. Because the products are custom-made to your home’s specifications, they cannot be returned. However, if you feel that you would rather measure yourself, be sure and use a sturdy, metal tape measure. Cloth tapes can stretch, and yardsticks are usually not long enough. Also, be sure to measure your windows at least twice and confirm your measurements with your spouse or a friend. We offer a free measurement service with all our blinds and home consultation.

Can you match what I already have in my home?

We can usually match your existing window coverings without a problem. We work with many different manufacturers and demand the same level of service with each and every one.

I can't find what I need. Can you help?

We are always happy to help. Our friendly team will always offer honest, helpful advice & where possible provide suitable solutions to your problem. Just give us a ring on 07341 645339. Alternatively, fill in our booking form, request a call back or drop us an email at blissfulblindsco369@gmail.com, and a member of our team will contact you as soon as possible.
