How Your Free Home Consultation Works

Every Leicester booking starts with a free, no-obligation home visit. One of our advisors comes to you, brings full fabric swatch books to browse in your own lighting, and takes exact professional measurements — so there's no need to measure your own windows or worry about getting it wrong.

Once you've chosen your fabric and style, a minimum deposit of 50% confirms and places your order, with the balance settled when the fitting team arrives (an adult over 18 needs to be present). We accept payment by bank transfer or PayPal; we don't currently accept cheques.

  • Average visit lasts 30 to 60 minutes
  • Instant, no-obligation quote, valid for 7 days
  • Blinds manufactured in 7 to 14 working days
  • Fitting typically arranged within 1 to 4 weeks
  • Evening and weekend appointments available on request
