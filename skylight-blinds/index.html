Skylight Blinds FAQs

Answers to common questions about our roof window blinds.

Which roof window brands do your skylight blinds fit?
Our skylight blinds are custom made to fit leading roof window manufacturers including Velux, Fakro, RoofLITE, and Keylite.
How do skylight blinds block out light completely for a loft bedroom?
Our blackout skylight blinds are fitted in side channels that seal the edges, completely blocking out angled sunlight, which is ideal for attic bedrooms and home cinemas.
How do I operate a blind on a hard-to-reach ceiling window?
Hard-to-reach ceiling blinds can be operated easily using an extension control rod, or you can choose a fully motorised remote control option.
How long does it take to have skylight blinds made and fitted?
Skylight blinds are custom-manufactured in 7-14 working days after your order is confirmed, with fitting timelines ranging from 1-4 weeks depending on the blind type and seasonal demand.
